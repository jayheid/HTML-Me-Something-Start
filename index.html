My Hawaii Vacation

My wife and I went to Hawaii for our Honeymoon in November 2023. We had both worked really hard with planning the wedding so we were eager to have a trip with plenty of exploration and relaxation to celebrate.

Big Island

Resort Life in Kailua-Kona

We began our trip with checking into our resort on Big Island. We enjoyed being able to see the mountain on the way to a hike each morning and relaxing by the pool with mai tai in hand for sunset.

Pololu Valley

We hiked in Pololu Valley on one of the first days in Big Island. The hike offered a great view of the coastline and a nice walk along the beach to view the valley.

Volcano National Park

We spent two days in Volcano National Park. We hiked around the park to look at the caldera, the lava tubes, and the rainforests surrounding the volcano. I was stuck by the extreme changes in environment from the desert in the immediate surrounding of the caldera and the lush rainforests that were were a bit beyond.

We had lodging at in an historic hotel in the park called Volcano House.One of my favorite features was a cozy fireplace which contained a fire that had never been extinguished. Much of my time relaxing before and after our hikes were spent reading in front of the fire.

Kauai

After our time on Big Island we traveled to Kauai. Because this was the oldest island, so it was much greener than we had previously seen in Big Island.

Na'Pali Coast

We did a boat tour around the Na'Pali Coast. We got to have great views of the coast line and snorkel in the water. We were also lucky enough to see dolphins on the cruise!

Kipu Valley

One of the highlights of the entire trip was ATV tour through the Kipu Valley, a cattle ranch that served as the location where several Hollywood movies were filmed. It was a thrilling experience driving through all of the jungles and seeing all of the beautiful sights that the ranch had to offer.
